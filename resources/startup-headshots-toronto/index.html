Startup Headshots
Toronto.

📅 Updated May 2026 ⏱ 5 min read ✍ SpeedyHeadshots Editorial

Startups have specific headshot needs that traditional photography studios aren't built for: fast turnaround, flexible booking for distributed or hybrid teams, consistent style across a roster that changes every few months, and pricing that makes sense before Series A. Here's how to handle it.

Why headshots matter more at early-stage

Counter-intuitive but true: the smaller and earlier-stage your startup, the more a professional team page matters. Investors, enterprise clients, and potential hires are all doing their own due diligence. A professional, consistent team page signals that you're organized, serious, and worth a meeting. Inconsistent headshots — some from phones, some clearly old, some obviously cropped from group photos — signal the opposite.

Your founding team's headshots will appear on your website, your pitch deck, press releases, conference speaker profiles, LinkedIn, and every piece of media coverage you get. They work harder than at almost any other company size.

The startup headshot problem

Traditional headshot photography is built around individuals booking solo sessions. Startups need something different:

  • Consistency across a team — everyone needs to look like they work at the same company, even if they booked at different times
  • Speed — you need images for a pitch or press announcement, not in 2 weeks
  • Scalability — when you hire 3 new engineers next month, they need to match the photos already on your team page
  • Pricing that works at small scale — you don't want to pay enterprise rates for 5 people

Our startup solution: We maintain a consistent backdrop and lighting setup that we can match session to session. When you hire, new team members can book individual sessions and receive photos that match your existing team page exactly.

What works for startup headshots

Style: professional but not stuffy

Most tech and startup teams lean business-casual or smart-casual — blazers are fine, ties rarely. The goal is to look credible and approachable without looking like a law firm. We'll match the formality level to your brand.

Backdrop: dark or clean mid-tone

Light grey or white backdrops are the classic corporate choice. Many startups prefer a dark backdrop (charcoal or near-black) — it reads as more modern and works especially well for teams where individuals have a range of skin tones and hair colours, since the contrast helps everyone pop.

Expression: real, not performed

Startup team pages that look great are the ones where the people in the photos look like people, not stock images. Genuine expressions — someone mid-laugh, a slight smile that reaches the eyes — land better than stiff professional grins. We're good at getting those out of people who've never been photographed professionally.

Logistics for team sessions

For teams of 5+, we come to your office or co-working space, set up a portable studio, and work through your team in a single half-morning. Sessions run roughly 15 minutes per person, so a team of 10 takes about 2.5 hours including setup.

Images are delivered within 24 hours. We maintain session notes and light settings so any future hires can match the existing photos exactly.
