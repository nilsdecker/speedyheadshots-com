How Much Do Headshots
Cost in Toronto? (2026)

📅 Updated May 2026 ⏱ 6 min read ✍ SpeedyHeadshots Editorial

The honest answer: headshots in Toronto range from $150 to $1,000+ depending on who you hire and what you need. Most people end up somewhere in the $250–550 range for a solid individual session at a reputable studio.

Here's what you're actually paying for, broken down by category.

Individual headshot pricing

For a single person booking a studio session, expect to pay in the following ranges:

Session type Typical range What's included
Mini / LinkedIn $200–300 20–30 min, 1 retouched image, all digitals
Professional $350–500 45–60 min, 2–3 retouched images, multiple looks
Executive $500–800 90–120 min, 4–5 retouched images, on-location option

At SpeedyHeadshots, our individual sessions run $250 (Mini), $385 (Professional), and $550 (Executive) — all including every digital image and 24-hour delivery.

Watch out for: Studios that charge per digital image on top of the session fee. All unretouched images from the session should be included as standard. Paying extra per file is outdated and unusual at quality studios.

Corporate team headshot pricing

Team headshot pricing works differently — it's charged per person, not per session, with the rate dropping as team size increases. Most Toronto studios charge between $100–200 per person for team shoots.

What should be included at any price point

Regardless of what you pay, these should come standard at any reputable Toronto headshot studio:

  • All unretouched digital images from the session
  • At least one professionally retouched image
  • Live preview on set (tethered camera or screen)
  • Posing guidance throughout
  • Choice of backdrop (white, black, textured)

Hidden costs to watch for

  • Per-image fees: Some studios charge $15–50 per digital image. Avoid — all digitals should be free.
  • Travel fees: For on-location or team shoots, confirm whether travel is included. Toronto-area surcharges of $50–200 are common.
  • Rush delivery surcharges: Standard 24h is included at SpeedyHeadshots. Some studios charge $50–100 to expedite.
  • Makeup and hair: Not included at most studios. Budget $100–250 separately if you want professional styling.
  • HST: All prices at Canadian studios are typically quoted before HST (13% in Ontario). Always confirm.

Are professional headshots worth the cost?

For professionals in client-facing roles, the answer is almost always yes. A LinkedIn profile with a professional headshot gets 21× more profile views and 9× more connection requests than one without. For a $250 investment that lasts 3–5 years, the math is straightforward.

For actors, it's non-negotiable — no professional submission package is complete without current industry-standard headshots. For corporate teams, a cohesive set of headshots is one of the clearest signals of a professional, credible organization.
