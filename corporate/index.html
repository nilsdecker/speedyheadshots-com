How It Works

Three steps.
Zero disruption.

01

Book & plan

Email us the headcount, preferred date, and office address. We'll confirm within a few hours and send a simple brief for your team — what to wear, what to expect, how long to block off in their calendar.

02

We set up at your office

We arrive 30 minutes early and set up a full professional studio in a spare meeting room or lobby. Lights, backdrop, tethered laptop for live preview. Your team walks in one at a time — no one needs to leave the building.

03

Images delivered in 24h

Every team member gets a private Google Drive link with their retouched image in web-optimized and full hi-res formats. Organized by person. Ready to upload to LinkedIn, your website, or your CMS the same day.
