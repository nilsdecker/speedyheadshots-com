Before You Book

What makes a great
LinkedIn photo.

Expression

Approachable, not stiff

The best LinkedIn photos have a natural, relaxed expression — confident without looking like a passport photo. We'll coach you through it. Most people are surprised how easy it is once the camera is rolling.

Wardrobe

Wear what you'd wear to work

A suit, smart casual, or whatever fits your industry. Solid colours work better than busy patterns. Avoid pure white if you're shooting against a light background. We'll send a simple prep guide when you book.

Background

Clean and distraction-free

We offer white, black, and textured backdrops. For LinkedIn, a clean light or medium grey is usually the strongest choice — it keeps attention on your face and works across every platform and device.

Framing

Head and shoulders

LinkedIn crops your photo to a circle in most views. We frame and retouch with this in mind — your face centred, shoulders visible, nothing cropped in an awkward spot.

Lighting

Professional, not harsh

We use studio-quality lights with large softboxes for natural, flattering light. No on-camera flash, no harsh shadows. The 61MP Sony sensor picks up every detail — which means our retouching has room to be subtle and natural.

Retouching

Real, just better

We remove minor blemishes and make light adjustments so you look like yourself on a good day — not an airbrushed version. The goal is a photo your colleagues will recognise when they meet you in person.
